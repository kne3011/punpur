Salat
  • Ricepe
    1. sausage - 100 g
    2. Hard cheese - 100 g
    3. Boiled eggs - 3 pcs
    4. Canned corn - 140 g
    5. Garlic - 1 clove
    6. Mayonnaise - 80 g
  • how to cook
    1. Dice the sausage.
    2. Dice the eggs
    3. Grate the cheese
    4. Squeeze the garlic through a garlic squeezer.
    5. Mix all the ingredients and season the hedgehog salad with mayonnaise.
Soup
  • Ricepe
    1. 2-3 potatoes
    2. processed cheese 2 pcs.
    3. chicken legs 1 pc.
    4. carrots 1 pc.
    5. vegetable oil
  • how to cook
    1. Boil potato cubes in boiling water, fry coarsely grated carrots with onion cubes. While preparing the roasting, cut chicken into pieces, grate frozen cheese.
    2. Pour the contents of the pan and meat to the potatoes, boil a little, add cheese, stirring constantly, let them dissolve completely. Serving a quick soup of processed cheese and ham is better with croutons from a loaf or, if you like, Borodino bread
